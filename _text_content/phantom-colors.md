## [EXPOSITION]
**'Phantom' complementary colors**

## [EXPOSITION]
Using the colormixer interface, select the shape and make it **Red**.

## [EXPOSITION]
Stare at it steadily for 8–10 seconds without looking away.

## [EXPOSITION]
When you're ready, click the background and then shift-select the Red shape to clone the background color.

## [EXPOSITION]
Still looking at the screen, you should see the phantom complementary color — **Cyan** — on the **Gray** background. (This effect works best on White and light Gray backgrounds.)

## [EXPOSITION]
The color is generated subconsciously by your brain!

## [EXPOSITION]
**Options:** Try this with any primary or secondary color.

**Bonus:** You can also try looking at a blank White wall after staring at the color for a few seconds. Same effect :)
